My time splits roughly across these areas, though the ratio shifts week to week:

**Automation & integration engineering** — I build the connective tissue

**Customer strategy** — I run reviews, build roadmaps, and act as the strategic
advisor for our larger accounts. I care about growing our strongest vertical.

**Compliance & security** — I'm standing up compliance-as-a-service as a core
offering: frameworks, hardening, and consulting across customer environments.

**Sales & business development** — I design the sales process, build proposals,
and set pricing. I sell against the customer's primary constraint rather than
leading with features.

**Internal operations** — I'm professionalizing our internal processes: ticket
lifecycle SOPs, dispatch procedures, change management, and role documentation.
